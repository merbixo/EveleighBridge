Experience the Divide

A practical demonstration of the current walking route between North and South Eveleigh.

Suggested Route

  1. Start at Carriageworks (North Eveleigh)
  2. Walk toward Redfern or Macdonaldtown crossing
  3. Continue via Erskineville access
  4. Return to South Eveleigh precinct

What the Route Demonstrates

  • Distance added by the rail corridor
  • Limited crossing points
  • Indirect access between adjacent precincts

A central bridge would provide a direct, visible connection between the two sides of the precinct.
