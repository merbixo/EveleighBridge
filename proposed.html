The Proposed Bridge

A direct pedestrian and cycle link aligned with reserved planning land on both sides of the rail corridor.

Location

South Landing: Locomotive Street between the Locomotive Workshop and Large Erecting Shop.

North Landing: South of the Paint Shop and Lifting Shed.

Technical Overview

  • Approximately 9 metres above active rail lines
  • Ramp and stair access
  • Fully accessible design
  • Potential integration with Wilson Street cycleway

Feasibility

Pedestrian bridges over active rail corridors are standard engineering practice in urban environments. Key considerations include rail clearance, heritage integration, and construction staging.
