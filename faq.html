Frequently Asked Questions

Why do we need a bridge now?
The area has transitioned from a closed industrial site to a dense residential and commercial precinct. The current detour is no longer sustainable for a walkable city.
Wasn’t this bridge already planned?
Yes, in 2004 the NSW Premier promised a bridge. While the surrounding land has been redeveloped, the critical pedestrian and cycle link was never built.
Will the bridge impact heritage buildings?
The design respects the NSW Heritage Register listing and provides enhanced access without compromising heritage values.
