The Proposed Bridge: Connecting the Precincts

The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance proposes a direct pedestrian and cycle link across Eveleigh. It aligns with the historic 1942 pedestrian bridge location and fits within the space reserved in current government renewal plans.

Technical Snapshot

  • Height: ~9 metres above active rail lines
  • Access: Ramps and stairs for prams, wheelchairs, bicycles
  • Integration: Harmonizes with heritage architecture
  • Connectivity: Links North and South Eveleigh efficiently

Route & Benefits

The bridge will connect Locomotive Street (South) to Carriageworks (North), shortening travel times for pedestrians and cyclists and reducing car dependency.
