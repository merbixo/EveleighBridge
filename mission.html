Our Mission

The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance advocates to turn the reserved space into a safe, modern pedestrian and cycle link, fulfilling the 2004 promise and connecting our community.

Build the Promised Link

The government’s renewal plans reserve space for a bridge but provide no plan to build it. We are campaigning to make this connection a reality for walkers, cyclists, and the community at large.
