Our Mission

The government’s renewal plans reserve space for a bridge but provide no plan to build it. The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance is working to turn that reserved space into a reality, creating a safe, modern pedestrian and cycle link that honors the 2004 promise.

Key Goals

  • Provide a safe and accessible pedestrian and cycle link.
  • Reconnect North and South Eveleigh for workers, students, and residents.
  • Support sustainable, active travel across the precinct.
  • Respect and enhance the heritage architecture of the site.
