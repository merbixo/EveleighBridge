Frequently Asked Questions

Answering the key questions about why the Eveleigh Bridge is needed, heritage impact, and community benefits.

Why do we need a bridge now?
With 20,000 workers, students, and cultural visitors, the current detour around the railway is no longer sustainable for a walkable city.
Wasn’t this bridge already planned?
Yes, promised in 2004, but never delivered. The land is reserved, but the connection is missing.
Will the bridge impact heritage buildings?
The design respects NSW Heritage Register listings and enhances access to the historic site.
Who is behind this campaign?
The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance, formed by ARAG, Friends of Erskineville, and REDWatch, along with local businesses and institutions.
How does this help traffic?
By making walking and cycling the fastest way across Eveleigh, we reduce short car trips and ease congestion on nearby roads.
Is there a spot for it?
Yes, current renewal plans have already reserved the location for the bridge.
