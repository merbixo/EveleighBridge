The Great Divide

The rail corridor between Redfern and Erskineville extends approximately 3.5 kilometres without a central pedestrian crossing.

Measured Impact

Example route: Carriageworks to South Eveleigh

  • Current route: approx. 16 minutes (1.2 km)
  • Direct bridge route: under 5 minutes

Transport Context

As development intensifies across both precincts, internal connectivity has not kept pace. A direct crossing supports active transport, reduces short car trips, and improves local accessibility.
