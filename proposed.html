The Proposed Bridge: Connecting the Precincts

The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance supports a practical pedestrian and cycle link at the centre of the precinct. The proposal reflects the location of the 1942 pedestrian bridge and improves day-to-day connectivity between the eastern and western sides of Eveleigh.

  • Height: Approx. 9 metres above rail corridor
  • Access: Ramps and stairs for accessibility compliance
  • Design: Integrated with heritage character
  • Purpose: Safe pedestrian and cycle connection
