The Great Divide

Understanding the physical and social barrier created by the rail corridor at Eveleigh, and why a bridge is critical for the community.

Barrier Overview

The rail corridor separating Redfern and Erskineville is 3.5 km long and cuts through multiple neighborhoods. Walking between north and south precincts requires a 46-minute detour, impacting residents, students, and businesses.

North Side Communities

  • Darlington
  • Newtown
  • Chippendale
  • Ultimo
  • Sydney University
  • Carriageworks

South Side Communities

  • Alexandria
  • Erskineville
  • Waterloo
  • South Eveleigh (CBA / Mirvac)
  • Local High School

Without a bridge, the corridor remains a barrier to community cohesion, commerce, and pedestrian safety.

Statistics at a Glance

  • 3.5 km – length of the rail corridor
  • 46 min – average detour for pedestrians
  • 100+ – businesses and institutions affected
  • 5000+ – residents directly impacted
