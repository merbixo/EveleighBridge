The Proposed Bridge

A direct pedestrian and cycle link connecting North and South Eveleigh, aligned with historic paths and current reserved land.

Connecting the Precincts

The bridge proposal sits almost exactly where a pedestrian bridge stood in 1942 and is aligned with current planning reserves. It ensures accessibility, safety, and heritage integration.

  • Height: ~9 metres above rail lines
  • Access: Ramps & stairs for prams, wheelchairs, and bicycles
  • Integration: Blends with heritage architecture of Carriageworks and Locomotive Workshops
