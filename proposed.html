The Proposed Bridge: Connecting the Precincts

The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance advocates for a direct pedestrian and cycle link at the heart of Eveleigh. This proposal aligns with the historic 1942 pedestrian bridge and improves connectivity across the precincts.

  • Height: ~9 metres above rail lines
  • Access: Ramps and stairs for full accessibility
  • Integration: Designed to blend with heritage architecture
