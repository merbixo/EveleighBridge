The Great Divide

The rail corridor between Redfern and Erskineville creates a 3.5 km physical barrier. Walking around takes 46 minutes, separating residents and workers, limiting access to schools, workplaces, and public transport.

Reconnecting Our Suburbs

  • North Side: Darlington, Newtown, Chippendale, Ultimo, Sydney University, Carriageworks
  • South Side: Alexandria, Erskineville, Waterloo, South Eveleigh (CBA/Mirvac), local high school

Impact of the Bridge

Journey Current With Bridge
Carriageworks → Large Erecting Shop 16 minutes Under 5 minutes
Full Barrier 3.5 km Reduced significantly
