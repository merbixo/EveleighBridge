Our Mission: Build the Promised Link

The NSW Government’s renewal plans reserve space for a bridge but provide no plan to build it. The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance advocates for a safe, modern pedestrian and cycle link that honors the 2004 promise and reconnects communities.
