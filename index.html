Bridging the Divide: The Eveleigh Bridge Project

For over a century, the railway corridor has divided Sydney’s inner suburbs. The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance advocates for a safe, modern pedestrian and cycle link reconnecting communities.

Support the Bridge View the Masterplan

The Barrier

Eveleigh has transformed from an industrial hub into a modern precinct, yet remains split by a 3.5 km rail corridor. Residents face long detours to access schools, jobs, and cultural sites.

A Legacy of Innovation

  • North Eveleigh: Carriageworks, cultural venues, 400,000+ annual visitors, social housing developments.
  • South Eveleigh: Tech hubs, Commonwealth Bank offices, CSIRO, Channel 7, 20,000+ daily workers.

The Broken Promise

In 2004, the NSW Government promised a bridge to reconnect these precincts. Two decades later, the connection has not been delivered.
