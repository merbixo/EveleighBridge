The Great Divide

The rail corridor between Redfern and Erskineville spans 3.5 km. Walking around it takes 46 minutes, separating communities and complicating daily trips.

Reconnecting Suburbs

  • North Side: Darlington, Newtown, Chippendale, Ultimo, Sydney University, Carriageworks
  • South Side: Alexandria, Erskineville, Waterloo, South Eveleigh (CBA/Mirvac), local high school

Time Saved

  • Carriageworks → Large Erecting Shop: 16 min now → < 5 min with bridge
