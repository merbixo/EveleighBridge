Eveleigh at a Glance

The Eveleigh precinct is a mix of residential, industrial, and heritage-listed buildings. It is currently divided by the rail corridor, which limits pedestrian and cyclist connectivity.

  • Dense residential and commercial growth on both sides of the corridor
  • Heritage buildings preserved but access limited
  • Current walking routes add significant time to daily journeys
