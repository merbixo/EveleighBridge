Our Mission

To ensure delivery of the pedestrian and cycle bridge site identified in planning documents connecting North and South Eveleigh.

Policy Context

Planning documents since 2004 have referenced a connection between the two precincts. Current renewal plans continue to reserve space for a bridge; however, delivery funding remains out of scope.

Objectives

  • Advocate for construction of the reserved bridge site
  • Support safe, accessible pedestrian and cycle infrastructure
  • Respect NSW Heritage Register requirements
  • Improve internal connectivity across the precinct
