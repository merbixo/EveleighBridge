The Great Divide: Why We Need the Bridge

The railway corridor between Redfern and Erskineville creates a 3.5 km physical barrier. A bridge will reconnect our neighborhoods, saving time and improving accessibility for all residents and workers.

Reconnecting Our Suburbs

  • North Side: Darlington, Newtown, Chippendale, Ultimo, Sydney University, Carriageworks.
  • South Side: Alexandria, Erskineville, Waterloo, South Eveleigh (CBA/Mirvac), local high school.

The "11-Minute Save"

  • Today: 1.2 km trek taking 16 minutes
  • With Bridge: Under 5 minutes

Growing Pains

  • Safe active travel for walkers and cyclists
  • Easy access to Eveleigh Markets, Carriageworks, and Sydney University
  • Reduced reliance on cars for short trips
