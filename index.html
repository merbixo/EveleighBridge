Bridging the Divide: The Eveleigh Bridge Project

For over a century, the railway corridor has served as the industrial heart of Sydney. Today, it stands as a physical barrier keeping neighbours apart. We are campaigning to finally build the bridge that will reunite across Eveleigh.

The Eveleigh Barrier

The rail corridor divides north and south Eveleigh, creating long detours for residents, workers, and students. Building a bridge reconnects the communities efficiently and safely.
