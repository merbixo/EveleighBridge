Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a bridge needed?

The rail corridor forms a 3.5km barrier between surrounding communities. The proposed pedestrian and cycle bridge would significantly reduce travel times and improve accessibility.

Will the bridge impact heritage sites?

The project is designed to respect and integrate with the historic Eveleigh Railway Workshops and surrounding heritage assets. Detailed heritage assessments would guide the final design.

Who is funding the project?

Funding would be subject to government planning approvals and infrastructure prioritisation processes. Community advocacy supports inclusion in future infrastructure programs.

Will it increase traffic?

The bridge is intended for pedestrians and cyclists only. It aims to reduce car dependency by providing a safe, direct alternative for local trips.

How can I support the project?

You can support the initiative by contacting local representatives, sharing information within your community, and signing up on our Support page.
