The Proposed Bridge: Connecting the Precincts

The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance advocates for a direct pedestrian and cycle link at the heart of Eveleigh. This proposal sits almost exactly where a pedestrian bridge stood in 1942.

Technical Snapshot

  • Height: ~9 metres above the rail lines
  • Access: Ramps and stairs for 100% accessibility
  • Integration: Designed to blend with heritage architecture
