Experience the Divide

Walk the perimeter of the rail corridor to see firsthand why a direct bridge is essential for connectivity.

Start at Carriageworks (North) and attempt to reach The Foundry (South) via Redfern, Macdonaldtown, and Erskineville crossings. Notice the detours and barriers.
