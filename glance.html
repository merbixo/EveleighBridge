Eveleigh at a Glance

North and South Eveleigh have evolved into cultural, residential, and tech hubs, separated by the railway corridor. Understanding each side helps visualize why a bridge is essential.

North Eveleigh

Primary Use: Arts, Culture, Housing

Daily Visitors: 400,000+

Nearby: Sydney University, Darlington Public School

South Eveleigh

Primary Use: Tech Hub & Innovation

Daily Workers: 20,000+

Nearby: Alexandria Park Community School, Waterloo Metro
