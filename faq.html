Frequently Asked Questions

Why do we need a bridge now?
The area is now a dense residential and commercial suburb. Walking around the barrier is no longer practical.
Wasn’t this bridge already planned?
Yes, it was promised in 2004. The land is reserved, but the bridge was never built.
Will the bridge impact heritage buildings?
The design respects NSW Heritage Register listings and improves public access.
How does this help traffic?
Encouraging walking and cycling reduces short car trips and eases local road congestion.
