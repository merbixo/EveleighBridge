Bridging the Divide

For over a century, the railway corridor has separated our neighbourhoods. We aim to build the Eveleigh Bridge to reconnect our community.

Join us in advocating for a safe, accessible pedestrian and cycle link. The bridge will reconnect key precincts while respecting heritage architecture.
