Bridging the Divide: The Eveleigh Bridge Project

For over a century, the railway corridor has served as the industrial heart of Sydney. Today, it stands as a physical barrier keeping neighbours apart. We are campaigning to finally build the bridge that will reunite across Eveleigh.

Support the Bridge View the Masterplan

The Great Divide: Why We Need the Bridge

The rail corridor separates north and south Eveleigh. Walking around the corridor takes 46 minutes, limiting access to schools, workplaces, and transport.

Reconnecting Our Suburbs

  • North Side: Darlington, Newtown, Chippendale, Ultimo, Sydney University, Carriageworks.
  • South Side: Alexandria, Erskineville, Waterloo, South Eveleigh (CBA/Mirvac), local high school.

Our Mission: Build the Promised Link

The government’s renewal plans reserve space for a bridge but provide no plan to build it. The Eveleigh Bridge Alliance advocates for a safe, modern pedestrian and cycle link.
