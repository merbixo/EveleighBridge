Experience the Divide

Walk the current paths and see the barriers created by the rail corridor. Observe how detours affect residents, commuters, and students.

The 46-minute detour around the rail corridor is a daily inconvenience. A pedestrian bridge would reconnect key precincts, improving accessibility and walkability.
